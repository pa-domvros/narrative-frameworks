The foundational blueprint for nearly every screenplay, novel, and play.

Introduce the protagonist and their world. An Inciting Incident shatters their status quo.

Plot Point 1

Act II: Confrontation

The protagonist pursues their goal, facing rising stakes and obstacles, leading to a Midpoint shift.

Plot Point 2

Act III: Resolution

The journey culminates in a final Climax, followed by the story's resolution and a glimpse of the new normal.

üíß Scene Structure (P-A-S)

A micro-framework for building tension and action within a single scene.

1. Problem

What is the character's immediate goal or obstacle in this scene? (e.g., "The door is locked.")

2. Agitate

What raises the stakes and makes the problem worse? (e.g., "Footsteps are approaching from behind.")

3. Solve

How does the character resolve the immediate problem, leading to the next scene? (e.g., "She breaks the lock with a crowbar.")
